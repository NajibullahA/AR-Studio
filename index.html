About me

I’m Aliteimor Samizada, an architect and designer with 4+ years of experience in architecture, landscape design, and visualization. I hold Bachelor’s and Master’s degrees in Architecture and Landscape from Satbayev University, where I built a strong foundation in creative and sustainable design.

I’ve collaborated with renowned studios such as Новые Дом, Elzat Muqash, Dream Studio, and Emaar Stahkom, contributing to diverse residential, commercial, and landscape projects. My approach blends functionality and aesthetic excellence, creating spaces that inspire and endure. Proficient in 3ds Max, AutoCAD, Revit, Corona, V-Ray, D5, Lumion, and Photoshop, I bring ideas to life with precision and creativity. Fluent in Dari, English, Russian, Kazakh, and Hindi, I thrive in multicultural teams and global projects.

My goal is to design innovative, sustainable, and visually captivating spaces that enrich the way people live and connect with their environment.

Services

01

Interior Design

We craft interiors that blend beauty, comfort, and purpose. Every space is thoughtfully designed to reflect your personality and lifestyle while ensuring seamless functionality. From concept development to material selection and visualization, we create timeless interiors that inspire and elevate everyday living.

02

Exterior Design

Our exterior designs balance form and function to create striking architectural identities. We focus on proportion, texture, and material harmony to bring your vision to life—whether it’s a modern façade, a classic renovation, or a full-scale architectural concept. Every detail is designed to enhance both aesthetic appeal and long-term performance.

03

Landscape Design

We design landscapes that connect people with nature through harmony, sustainability, and creativity. From private gardens to large outdoor spaces, our approach integrates natural beauty with practical use, ensuring each environment feels inviting, balanced, and alive. The result is an outdoor experience that complements and completes the architecture.
